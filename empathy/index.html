Empathy

What is Empathy? Empathy is my small business, the purpose of Empathy is to give homeless people the basic things needed for survival such as food, shelter, clothing, etc.

Problem Worth Solving Homeless people can't afford to buy food, clothing, a house, etc. It's hard to watch homeless people on the streets, sometimes with kids begging for money. They need a safe place to live, they don't have shelter and sometimes they don't even have clothes. Most of the time homeless people have to dig in the garbage to find certain things they need. They're always worried about where the next meal is coming from if it's even a meal and how they're going to afford that meal.

Why Should You Help?

  1. It makes you feel good!
  2. They appreciate it and it gives them hope
  3. It's your chance to make a difference
  4. It's so easy to help
